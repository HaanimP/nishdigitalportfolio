I'm Ni-shaat Gafieldien, a design-focused web developer based in Cape Town, South Africa. I specialise in creating responsive websites, brand-focused digital experiences, and strategic online solutions that help businesses grow.

With a background spanning digital design, social media management, SEO, and brand strategy, I take a holistic approach to digital development translating client visions into functional, visually appealing platforms that enhance user engagement and strengthen brand identity.
